The Best Movies According To Freddy

My top 3 movies of all-time

Gladiator

An incredible time piece that tells of a brilliant general that was set to inherit the Roman Empire but then is betrayed and ends up as a gladiator in Rome.

Shawshank Redemption

The greatest movie of all time on IMBD. It's a story about a man wrongly accused and sentenced to prison and how he finds his path to redemption.

Fight Club

This movie introduces a deadbeat insomniac who meets a mysterious stranger on a plane and ends up twisted into a terrorist group set on disrupting the world as we know it.
